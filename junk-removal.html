Our Simple 4-Step Process

Getting rid of your junk has never been easier. Here's how we make it happen:

1

Schedule Your Appointment

Call us at (305) 986-0692 or book online. We offer flexible scheduling including same-day and next-day appointments. Our team will provide an estimated price range.

2

We Arrive On Time

Our professional crew arrives in uniform with a clean truck. We'll call 30 minutes before arrival and provide an upfront, no-obligation estimate based on truck space used.

3

We Do All The Work

Point to what you want removed and we'll handle the rest. We provide all labor, from anywhere in your home or property. No need to move items to the curb.

4

Eco-Friendly Disposal

We sort, recycle, and donate whenever possible. Items in good condition go to local charities. Everything else is disposed of responsibly at licensed facilities.

What We Remove

We handle all types of junk removal, big or small. Here's what we commonly remove:

Household Items

  • Furniture (sofas, chairs, tables, beds)
  • Appliances (refrigerators, washers, dryers)
  • Electronics (TVs, computers, stereos)
  • Mattresses and box springs
  • Clothing and personal items
  • Books, magazines, and papers

Construction & Renovation

  • Drywall and plaster debris
  • Flooring materials (carpet, tile, wood)
  • Cabinets and countertops
  • Windows and doors
  • Roofing materials
  • Concrete and brick (small amounts)

Yard & Outdoor

  • Tree branches and logs
  • Leaves and grass clippings
  • Garden furniture and equipment
  • Pool equipment and accessories
  • Outdoor playsets and structures
  • Landscaping materials

Commercial & Office

  • Office furniture and equipment
  • Filing cabinets and documents
  • Restaurant equipment
  • Retail fixtures and displays
  • Industrial equipment
  • Warehouse cleanouts

Items We Cannot Remove:

Hazardous materials, paint, chemicals, asbestos, medical waste, tires, and automotive fluids. Contact us if you're unsure about specific items.
